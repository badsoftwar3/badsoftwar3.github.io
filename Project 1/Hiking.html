Glacier Experience is a small group experience than offers an easy glacier hike on Solhemimajokull glacier with an experienced glacier guide and is available all year around.The tour offers a pick-up from Rekjavik or a meet on location


Solheimajokull glacier is an outlet from the mighty Myrdalsjokull and is surrounded by some of Iceland's most grand volcanoes and during summertime, a glacier lagoon forms in front of the glacier only adding to the charm of the location. This popular day tour is the perfect way to get in real contract with the true reason behind the name Ice-land!
